Overview

Heatwaves are prolonged periods of excessively hot weather which may be accompanied by high humidity. They increase the risk of heat-related illness, especially for older adults, children and people with chronic illnesses.

Simple Actions

  • Stay hydrated and avoid strenuous activity during peak heat.
  • Move to air-conditioned areas when possible.
  • Check on neighbors and vulnerable people.

Campus Tips

  1. Know cooling centers and shaded areas on campus.
  2. Keep emergency contact numbers and medical info accessible.
  3. Report heat-related incidents to campus health services.
