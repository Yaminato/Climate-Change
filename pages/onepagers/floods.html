Overview

Floods can happen quickly during heavy rains or storms. Know the local flood risk and nearest high ground.

Before & During

  • Learn evacuation routes and shelter locations.
  • Move valuables to higher shelves and unplug electronics if safe.
  • Do not walk or drive through floodwaters.

Campus Tips

  1. Familiarize yourself with campus assembly points and evacuation routes.
  2. Keep a small grab-and-go kit with essentials.
